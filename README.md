## 📖 What is WRLDX6900?

The Solana DeFi ecosystem is highly fragmented. Traders constantly rotate between individual tickers, hoping to catch the next trend. **WRLDX6900** solves this fragmentation by introducing the concept of a **World Index** to the memecoin ecosystem.

Instead of guessing which single ticker will pump, you trade the global macro narrative. 

**$WRLDX** serves as the "headline" index—a single token that represents a curated basket of the world's most significant market indices (e.g., the S&P 500, Nikkei 225, Hang Seng). All tokens in the WRLDX ecosystem are fair-launched via Pump.fun, ensuring zero insider allocations and pure price discovery.

## ⚙️ The Ecosystem Architecture

WRLDX6900 is powered by a transparent, decentralized, and verifiable infrastructure:

### 1. The Headline ($WRLDX)
The primary token of the ecosystem. Holding `$WRLDX` gives you exposure to the aggregate performance of the entire constituent basket and grants you access to community governance.

### 2. The Basket (Constituents)
Individual tokens representing regional indices (e.g., `$SPX`, `$NDX`, `$NKY`, `$HSI`). These are traded independently but feed into the overarching **WRLDX World Level**.

### 3. The World Level Aggregator
Our open-source engine tracks the live prices of all constituent tokens and calculates the **WRLDX World Level**—a single, dynamic integer representing the aggregate "health" of the global market basket.

## 🔄 The 50/25/25 Economic Flywheel

The true power of WRLDX6900 lies in its hardcoded fee distribution mechanism. Every constituent token generates trading fees, which are sent to the public WRLDX Treasury. 

These fees are deployed automatically:
- 🟢 **50% Buyback ($WRLDX):** Used to continuously market-buy the headline token, creating structural upward pressure.
- 🔵 **25% Buyback (Constituents):** Distributed to buy back the individual tokens in the basket, maintaining their liquidity and health.
- 🟡 **25% Growth & Operations:** Allocated for infrastructure, aggregator hosting, and ecosystem expansion.

## 🏛️ Community Governance (The World Desk)

The WRLDX basket is not static. It expands based on the will of the community. Holders of the `$WRLDX` token unlock governance tiers to propose and vote on new index constituents (e.g., KOSPI, SENSEX) to be launched into the ecosystem.

- **Public Tier:** View the live World Level and track the Treasury Watcher logs.
- **Macro Tier (≥ 1% Supply):** Debate and vote on new market additions.
- **Whale Tier (≥ 3% Supply):** Directly propose new constituent tickers for the community to vote on.
